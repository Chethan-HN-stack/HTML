WELCOME TO CHN RESTAURANT

CHN Restaurant is a popular South Indian restaurant known for its authentic and flavorful food. It serves traditional dishes such as dosa, idli, vada, and sambar prepared with fresh ingredients. The restaurant focuses on providing healthy and hygienic meals to its customers. Coconut, curry leaves, and aromatic spices are widely used to enhance the taste of the food. CHN Restaurant offers both vegetarian and non-vegetarian South Indian dishes. The atmosphere is simple, clean, and welcoming for families and individuals. Serving food with warmth and care is an important value of the restaurant. Filter coffee is one of the special highlights enjoyed by many customers. The restaurant reflects the rich culture and tradition of South Indian cuisine. CHN Restaurant is loved for its quality food and comforting dining experience.
